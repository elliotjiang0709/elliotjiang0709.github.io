Who are we ?

Elliot & Co., is a London's independent investment bank and financial services company. Apart from investment banking, it offers services in investment management, securities, asset management, prime brokerage, and securities underwriting.

Elliot & was founded in London in 2017 by Elliot Jiang. In 2018, Henry Karl joined the firm. In the same year, Yip Tsang, Arya and John also joined the business and the firm adopted its present name, Elliot & Co. The company made a name for itself pioneering the use of commercial paper for entrepreneurs and joined the London Stock Exchange (LSE) in 2018. By 2018, the firm's capital stood at $1.6 million, and was growing rapidly.
